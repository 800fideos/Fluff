Título: Fluff! Por los pelos

Texto corto:
Fluffs have spread out all around the house! 
Help them to find other fluffs in the same room to join and form a mega fluff. 

Texto largo:
Fuff! Por los pelos features 25 levels. The adventure begins in the attic, passing through the living room, kitchen and many other corners of the house. 
While the task of forming the megafluff requires a simple swipe of the finger, 
determining the best approach and snagging the most stars is where the game truly entertains.
This game has been developed by senior game development students.
